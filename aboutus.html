About Us

NRI Mitra serves as a reliable companion for NRIs, offering a comforting presence for those living away from their home country. Acting as a vital link between the Indian diaspora abroad and their roots in India, we are dedicated to tending to your requirements, allowing you to pursue your endeavors overseas with peace of mind. Our services encompass comprehensive support, including the provision of skilled nurses and regular health checkups for elderly parents. Additionally, we offer dependable domestic assistance to aid them in their daily tasks. Managing property affairs, we oversee leases with tenants and provide maintenance services for your residence.
